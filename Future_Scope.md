# SprintIQ -- Future Scope

## Vision

SprintIQ aims to evolve into an enterprise-grade **AI Scrum Copilot**
that assists Scrum Masters, Product Owners, Business Analysts,
Developers, and QA Engineers throughout the Agile software development
lifecycle. The platform will combine AI-driven insights, Jira
automation, intelligent reporting, and human-in-the-loop decision-making
to improve planning accuracy, productivity, and delivery confidence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 2 -- Jira Automation

-   Epic management (Create, Update, Retrieve, Link Stories)
-   Sprint lifecycle management
-   Board and backlog management
-   Version and release management
-   Issue linking and dependency management
-   Attachments and worklogs
-   Watchers and labels
-   Bulk Jira operations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 3 -- AI Agents

Introduce specialized AI agents for:

-   Velocity & Performance Analysis
-   Capacity Planning
-   Dependency Analysis
-   Technical Debt Detection
-   QA & Test Case Generation
-   Release Readiness Assessment
-   Sprint Health Monitoring
-   Sprint Retrospective Generation

## Long-Term Vision

SprintIQ will evolve into a comprehensive AI-powered Scrum Copilot that
supports Agile teams from backlog refinement to sprint completion. By
combining intelligent multi-agent workflows, Jira automation, enterprise
integrations, predictive analytics, and explainable AI, SprintIQ aims to
become a scalable platform that accelerates software delivery while
ensuring transparency, governance, and human oversight.
